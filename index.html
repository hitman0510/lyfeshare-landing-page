About LyfeShare

LyfeShare is an innovative platform designed to securely store, manage, and share important documents, information, and files within a trusted network of family and close friends. It empowers users to safeguard their legacy, simplify document management, and ensure that critical information is readily accessible when needed. Beyond just storage, LyfeShare offers a nurturing community space where users can access resources such as bereavement support, legal and financial advisory services, and educational webinars.

LyfeShare's patented technology encapsulates a distinctive system designed for the secure storage, management, and selective sharing of essential documents and legacy-related information. It details a sophisticated mechanism that allows users to not only organize their digital assets but also to specify controlled access to these assets for their trusted network, either immediately or on a time release schedule. This patent safeguards the proprietary method of LyfeShare, ensuring a secure and user-centric approach to managing and passing on important personal and legal documents within a closed network.

How LyfeShare Works

Create Your Account

An individual will create a Lyfe Share Vault account, this individual will be known as the Prime Account Holder.

Account Verification Period

The account verification period is the time interval you establish to confirm your continued control over your account. Failing to complete this verification prompts LyfeShare to initiate a verification process, beginning with contacting your designated Sub-Prime Account Holder, should any concerns arise about your well-being or account's security.

Profile Creation and Syncing

Begin your LyfeShare journey with a simple, secure profile setup. Enter your basic information, engage robust security features like multi-factor authentication, and start managing your important documents with ease and peace of mind.

Folder Creation and Syncing

Easily organize your documents with LyfeShare's intuitive folder creation feature. Just choose a category, name your folder, and start organizing your files for streamlined access and management.

Buddy setup

Connect and collaborate securely with LyfeShare's Buddy Invitation process. Simply send an invite to family or friends, granting them access to shared documents, ensuring seamless collaboration within your trusted circle.

Account Managing and Syncing

All that’s left for you to do is effortlessly manage your LyfeShare account with user-friendly tools designed for simplicity and efficiency. Update personal information, customize security settings, and control document access, all within a few clicks for a personalized experience.
